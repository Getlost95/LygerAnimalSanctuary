About Us

Lyger Animal Sanctuary, located in the scenic town of Pililla, Rizal, Philippines, is a safe haven for exotic and rescued animals. Our sanctuary is home to majestic ligers, powerful tigers, gentle lions, camels, colorful birds, and fascinating reptiles. We are committed to providing a responsible and educational environment where visitors of all ages can safely interact with and learn about these incredible creatures.

Founded with a passion for wildlife conservation and animal welfare, Lyger Animal Sanctuary strives to inspire awareness and appreciation for the natural world. We provide guided tours, interactive experiences, and educational programs that showcase the unique behaviors, habitats, and care of our animals. Our staff is highly trained and dedicated to ensuring both animal well-being and visitor safety, making every visit both memorable and meaningful.

Beyond offering fun and educational encounters, our sanctuary actively participates in rescue and rehabilitation programs. Many of our animals have been rescued from unsafe situations and are given a second chance at life here. By visiting Lyger Animal Sanctuary, guests directly support our mission to protect wildlife and promote conservation initiatives locally and globally.

Whether you are an animal lover, a family looking for a fun day out, or a student eager to learn more about wildlife, Lyger Animal Sanctuary promises an unforgettable experience. Come and explore the wonders of wildlife, witness the beauty of exotic animals, and join us in our journey to make a positive impact on the world of animal conservation.
